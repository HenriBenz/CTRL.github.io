# CTRL e.V. — Construction Technology and Robotics Laboratory e.V.

Official website repository for **CTRL e.V.**, a student-driven initiative accredited by **RWTH Aachen University**.  
We are an interdisciplinary platform of students and academic contributors working on **construction robotics** and the **digitalization of construction processes** — from research and prototyping to real-world applications.

## About CTRL e.V.

The ongoing digital transformation is fundamentally reshaping the construction industry.  
**CTRL e.V. (Construction Technology and Robotics Laboratory e.V.)** exists to actively strengthen and advance research, teaching, and student initiatives in the fields of **construction robotics**, **automation**, and **digital construction workflows**.

We connect:
- Students across disciplines (civil engineering, robotics, computer science, architecture, etc.)
- Scientific staff and research groups
- Industry partners and the wider public

We create room for **creative solutions**, **experimental approaches**, and **technological innovation** in construction automation.

## What we do

- Prototype and test robotics + automation concepts for construction
- Support student projects and research initiatives
- Organize workshops, talks, demos, and lab sessions
- Build bridges between university, industry, and society
